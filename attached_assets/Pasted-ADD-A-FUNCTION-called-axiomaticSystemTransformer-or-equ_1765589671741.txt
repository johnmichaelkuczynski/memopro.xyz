**PROCESSING LOGIC:**

The function should use an LLM call with a system prompt instructing it to:

1. First read the entire input text and identify its central thesis
2. Identify what ontological categories are being posited (these become sorts)
3. Identify what entities are being named as fundamental (these become constants)
4. Identify what properties and relations are being attributed (these become predicates)
